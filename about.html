ABOUT US ABOUT US ABOUT US

TO BE COMPLETELY HONEST, PEDRO WAS STARTED IN THE BACK OF A U-HAUL VAN, RUNNING ON 2 HOURS OF SLEEP AND COVERED IN SAND. THERE NEEDED TO BE A BETTER WAY TO THRIVE IN THE INDUSTRY WE LOVED SO MUCH

Underpaid (the job we were on was a favor) and overworked, we’ve witnessed first hand the untapped creative potential of the events and floral labor force. We wanted to employ and work alongside with the true powerhouses… and thus UnderNewMGMT was born.

We at UnderNewMGMT seek to shift traditional industry power structures by exclusively hiring and collaborating with individuals from the POC, immigrants, women, and members of the LGBTQ+ communities.

We seek to take up as much bright, loud space as possible.

We are as much dedicated to the floral world as we are to providing creative & long-lasting solutions to help maintain our beautiful local communities. We currently run a mutual aid project The People’s Bodega - which provides nutrition and protest supplies wherever needed. We have active projects working in the Los Angeles area and New York area.

We constantly develop new designs that utilize vintage and recycled materials to provide our clients with unique, sustainable event and floral options. We avoid using single use floral foam and other non-renewable materials whenever possible. Our events seek to tell stories and uplift communities.

Thank you for being part of this journey

xoxo

Alexandra Floro

Owner + Principal Designer
